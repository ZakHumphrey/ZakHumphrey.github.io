Benefits to Vista Students

Our mission: Providing stress relief to MVHS students through the power of positivity and a therapy dog. 

Mental Benefits

-DECREASE in stress and anxiety, depression, loneliness and feelings of isolation, aggressive behaviors

-INCREASE in feelings of acceptance, ability to accept social and emotional support, socialization, opportunities for laughter, mental stimulation, attention, SPIRIT & SELF-ESTEEM

-A Great way to bring the school together with positivity!

Physical Benefits

-DECREASE in blood pressure, heart rate, stress hormone cortisol

-INCREASE in hormones associated with health and well-being, fitness, fine motor skills
